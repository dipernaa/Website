Burrito Bowl

Ingredients

Brown rice Lime
Cilantro Chicken
Onion Favorite hot pepper of choice
Chipotle peppers in adobo sauce Black beans
Corn Salsa
Oil Cumin
Garlic powder Salt & pepper

Rice

  1. Cook rice as directed on package
  2. Squeeze lime over rice and add chopped cilantro

Chicken

  1. Cook chicken in pan with oil
  2. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and cumin
  3. Add onions and your favorite hot pepper of choice halfway through
  4. Cook until chicken is browned
  5. Add crushed chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, black beans, and corn to pan

Serve

  1. Serve chicken on rice with salsa

Source: Connor Henke, Skyler Hokkanen

Baked Gnocchi

Ingredients

Potato gnocchi Basil leaves
Diced mozzarella cheese Tomato pasta sauce
Shredded parmesan cheese Salt

Gnocchi

  1. Bring pot of water, with salt, to a rolling boil
  2. Add gnocchi, when they are floating they are done
  3. Drain the gnocchi and put into a casserole dish
  4. Add the tomato pasta sauce and diced mozzarella, mix
  5. Sprinkle shredded parmesan cheese to top
  6. Throw in the oven at 350Â°F until parmesan cheese is melted and golden brown

Serve

  1. Garnish with basil leaves
